Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

Overview

Pyramid Push is a workout timer app that runs entirely on your device. Your privacy is important to us, and we've designed the app to respect it.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All workout settings and progress stay on your device and are never sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or any third-party services that collect data about you.

Local Storage

The app may store your preferences (like peak reps setting) locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on GitHub.

Common Questions

How does the pyramid work?
You do sets of 1, 2, 3... up to your peak, then back down. A peak of 10 means 100 total push-ups.

Can I change the peak?
Yes! Tap the number in the center of the screen before starting to adjust your peak.

Does it sync with Apple Health?
Yes, workouts are automatically saved to Apple Health when you complete them.
